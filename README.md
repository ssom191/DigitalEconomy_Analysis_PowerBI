# 🌐 Digital Economy & Innovation Trends Analysis

## Overview

A Power BI data visualisation project exploring digital economy and innovation trends across South Korea, Brazil, and New Zealand between 2013–2023 using World Bank Open Data.

The project analyses indicators related to:

* internet usage
* mobile and broadband connectivity
* ICT goods trade
* secure internet infrastructure
* research & development (R&D) investment

Using Power BI and Power Query, the project compares how digital infrastructure and innovation investment contribute to economic growth, connectivity, and technological competitiveness across developing and developed economies.

---

## Objectives

* Analyse South Korea’s digital economy and innovation landscape
* Compare South Korea’s technological growth against Brazil and New Zealand
* Explore long-term trends in digital connectivity and infrastructure
* Present data-driven insights through interactive Power BI storytelling

---

## Data Sources

### World Bank Open Data

The analysis uses publicly available World Bank indicators for South Korea, Brazil, and New Zealand related to:

* internet usage
* mobile cellular subscriptions
* fixed broadband subscriptions
* ICT goods exports and imports
* R&D expenditure
* secure internet servers

---

## Data Preparation & Transformation

Data preparation was performed entirely within Power Query in Power BI.

### Key Steps

* Cleaned and transformed World Bank datasets
* Filtered indicators relevant to digital economy and innovation
* Merged country datasets for comparative analysis
* Created derived measures and trend indicators
* Structured data for interactive visualisation and year-based filtering

---

## Key Areas of Analysis

### South Korea Digital Economy Analysis

* Growth in internet usage and mobile connectivity
* Expansion of secure internet infrastructure
* Trends in ICT imports, exports, and R&D investment

### South Korea vs Brazil

* Comparison of digital adoption and connectivity growth
* Differences in ICT trade and innovation investment
* Infrastructure and internet accessibility trends

### South Korea vs New Zealand

* Comparison between highly connected developed economies
* Differences in R&D intensity and digital infrastructure growth
* ICT trade and technological competitiveness analysis

---

## Key Insights

* South Korea demonstrated strong growth in digital infrastructure, secure internet systems, and mobile connectivity between 2013–2023.
* South Korea significantly outperformed Brazil in ICT exports, internet infrastructure, and innovation investment.
* New Zealand showed high internet accessibility and stable digital growth, though South Korea maintained a stronger ICT trade position.
* Increased R&D expenditure appeared closely aligned with growth in secure internet infrastructure and digital connectivity.
